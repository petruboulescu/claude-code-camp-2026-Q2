## Mechanics Learned
- `score` — shows level, exp-to-next-level, hp/mana/move, gold, alignment, quest points.
- `inventory` — lists carried items.
- Shops: `read sign`/`read note`, `list`, `buy <item>`, `sell <item>`, `value <item>`. No haggling, no credit.
- Guild inner doors reject you outright ("guard humiliates you") rather than prompting for a password/class check — cause not yet diagnosed (needs guild join command? right alignment? right race/class?).
